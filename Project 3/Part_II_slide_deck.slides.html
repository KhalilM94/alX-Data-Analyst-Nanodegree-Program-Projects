Investigation Overview¶

The Ford Gobike dataset and subsequent studies revealed a number of intriguing facts about the distribution of bike journeys taken within the chosen time period. Explorations of these links have allowed me to see how seniors enjoy their journeys; primarily not sharing; how much time they spend on excursions; not particularly long; and to respond to other questions.

Dataset Overview¶

The present document examines a dataset that includes roughly 183,412 trips from a Ford GoBike. This data collection contains details on each ride taken in a bike-sharing programme that serves the greater San Francisco Bay area.
